What is MYBOOKFOLIO?

MyBookfolio is a Battalion Management System that renders all Army JROTC programs with the tools necessary to manage, maintain, and overview their Battalions progress throughout the year with accordance to the JROTC program of accreditation. Our powerful platform will help you achieve your overall battalion goal and will also provide you with the confidence you need in knowing that you are partnered with the most reliable and secure Battalion Management System. Our platform includes a cadet, staff, and instructor profile each made to accommodate the needs of your battalion.
